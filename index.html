Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Temu Data Extractor Chrome extension ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Information We Access

Our extension accesses the following information:

  • Public Product Data: When you click the "Scrape Data" button on a Temu.com page, we access publicly visible product information including:
    • Product titles, prices, and descriptions
    • Ratings and review counts
    • Product images and specifications
    • Available colors, sizes, and variants
    • Seller/store information
  • Browser Storage: We use Chrome's local storage to save your scraping preferences and extracted data between sessions.

3. How We Use Information

We use the accessed information solely for:

  • Extracting product data from Temu.com as requested by you
  • Saving your scraping preferences locally in your browser
  • Formatting data for export to CSV, Excel, or JSON files
  • Providing data filtering and analysis features within the extension

4. Data Storage & Processing

Important: All data processing occurs locally in your browser. We do not:

  • Send any data to external servers
  • Store your extracted data on our servers
  • Collect or process personal information
  • Share data with third parties

Data is stored locally using Chrome's storage API and persists only until you clear it or uninstall the extension.

5. Permissions Justification

Our extension requires the following permissions:

  • activeTab: To access the current Temu.com tab when you click our icon
  • storage: To save your preferences and extracted data locally
  • scripting: To execute content scripts on Temu.com pages
  • *.temu.com: To access Temu product pages across all regional domains

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party libraries:

  • SheetJS (xlsx.js): For Excel export functionality. This library runs locally in your browser for file generation only.

7. Your Rights & Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • Clear all stored data anytime using the "Clear Data" button in the extension
  • Choose what data to extract and export
  • Uninstall the extension at any time, which removes all locally stored data

8. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
